How to Book Tickets


Step 1: Sign Up.
Step 2: Then Login with your credentials (i.e., Username and password).
Step 3: Enter Station between which you want to travel and date of journey
Step 4: You have to select No. Of passengers and Class of Birth (Economy or Business).
Step 5: Select preferable flight :
a) Make payment
b) After payment, you get PNR Number
c) Print Ticket
d) You can check also PNR Status Through PNR Check on the Home Page

About Airline
